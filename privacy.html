Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

SwaadSe.in (“we”, “our”) operates a homemade meal ordering website. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Information we collect

  • Account: email address and authentication identifiers (via Firebase Auth).
  • Orders: name, phone, delivery address, order items, and special instructions.
  • Payments: payment status and transaction references from Razorpay (we do not store full card numbers).
  • Technical: basic analytics and security logs (e.g. Firebase Analytics, App Check) where enabled.

How we use data

  • To prepare and deliver your meals
  • To contact you about orders (phone, email, or WhatsApp)
  • To improve our menu and service
  • To prevent fraud and secure our systems

Storage & security

Data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore) with access controlled by security rules. Only authenticated users can access their own orders. Admin access is limited to authorized staff accounts.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with service providers required to run the site (Firebase, Razorpay, hosting) under their terms.

Your rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your account data by contacting us through the website after signing in.

Cookies

We use essential cookies/local storage for cart and sign-in sessions. Analytics cookies may be used if enabled in production.

Contact

Use the contact section on our homepage (visible after sign-in) or the email configured for support.
